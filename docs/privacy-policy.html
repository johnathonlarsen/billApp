Family Bank Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Who we are

Family Bank is a private bill-tracking app for our household. It is not a bank and does not hold deposits. We built it for family use only — not for the public.

What we collect

On your phone (locally): bills you enter, bank/account labels, and payment records when you mark bills paid.

Via Plaid (optional): account names and last-four digits, balances, and transaction descriptions, amounts, and dates (up to 90 days). Bank login is handled by Plaid — we never see or store your bank password.

On our shared server: anonymous Plaid usage counters and access tokens stored server-side only. We do not store bill names, amounts, or personal identifiers in the cloud.

How we use data

To show bills, send reminders, optionally match transactions to bills, and track Plaid usage limits. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Plaid

We use Plaid Inc. to connect to financial institutions. Plaid's privacy policy applies to bank connections: Plaid End User Privacy Policy. You can also manage connections at my.plaid.com.

Where data is stored

Bills stay on your phone. Plaid tokens and usage counters are on Supabase (encrypted at rest). Each family member's phone has its own local bill data.

Retention and removing Plaid

  • Local bill data stays until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Uninstalling the app does not remove your Plaid connection from our server — you can restore the saved link on a new install without relinking.
  • Remove Plaid connection (Banks screen, family password required) revokes the link with Plaid, deletes the server token, and unlinks the bank on your phone. Local accounts and cached transactions remain as labels only.
  • Removing a connection does not refund Plaid Trial slots on Plaid's billing plan.
  • API usage counters reset monthly.

Your choices

  • Use the app without Plaid (manual bills only).
  • Restore a saved Plaid link after reinstalling the app.
  • Permanently remove a Plaid connection with the password-protected option in the Banks screen.
  • Delete bills, banks, and payment history in the app.
  • Contact us to request deletion of server-side Plaid data.

Children

This app is for adult family members managing household bills. It is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do.
