Family Bank Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Who we are

Family Bank is a private bill-tracking app for our household. It is not a bank and does not hold deposits. We built it for family use only — not for the public.

What we collect

On your phone (locally): bills you enter, bank/account labels, and payment records when you mark bills paid.

Via Plaid (optional): transaction data to help mark bills paid. Bank login is handled by Plaid — we never see or store your bank password.

On our shared server: anonymous Plaid usage counters and access tokens stored server-side only. We do not store bill names, amounts, or personal identifiers in the cloud.

How we use data

To show bills, send reminders, optionally match transactions to bills, and track Plaid usage limits. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Plaid

We use Plaid Inc. to connect to financial institutions. Plaid's privacy policy applies to bank connections: Plaid End User Privacy Policy. You can manage connections at my.plaid.com.

Where data is stored

Bills stay on your phone. Plaid tokens and usage counters are on Supabase. Each family member's phone has its own local bill data.

Your choices

Use the app without Plaid, disconnect banks anytime, and delete your data in the app.
